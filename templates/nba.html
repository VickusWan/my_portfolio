Fantasy Basketball Dashboard

Fantasy basketball is a game where participants acts as owners/ GM of a hypothetical professional basketball team. Each competitors in the league select their rosters by participating in a draft in which all National Basketball Association (NBA) players are available. Fantasy points are awarded based on the actual performances of the drafted basketball players in real-world competition. Fantasy points are calculated with the following:

  • For each point scored: 1
  • For each rebound: 1.2
  • For each assist: 1.5
  • For each block: 2
  • For each steal: 2
  • For each turnover: -1
